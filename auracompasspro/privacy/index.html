Privacy Policy

Clear, focused data use for field navigation features.

Last updated: May 22, 2026

Aura Compass Pro is designed to deliver compass, waypoint, track, and route tools with the least amount of data handling necessary to support the experience.

Information the App Uses

  • Location data to show coordinates, heading context, distance, waypoints, and route tracking.
  • Motion data to improve compass stability, heading behavior, and inclinometer features.
  • User-created content such as waypoints, track sessions, notes, and imported GPX files.

How Data Is Used

  • Location and motion data are used to power the core navigation experience.
  • Waypoints, track history, and related metadata are stored on the device for user access.
  • Widget and Apple Watch companion surfaces may read shared snapshot data through the configured App Group.
Aura Compass Pro does not share your routes, saved locations, or motion data with third parties unless you explicitly export or share content yourself.

GPX Import and Export

GPX files are imported or exported only when initiated by the user. Exported files are shared through Apple system share sheets or saved by the user to a destination they choose.

Retention and Deletion

Saved waypoints, sessions, and related metadata remain on your device until you delete them or remove the app. If you use the widget or Apple Watch companion, shared snapshot data is updated to reflect the latest state made available by the app.

Children's Privacy

Aura Compass Pro is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
